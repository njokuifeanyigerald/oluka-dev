Dr. Frank Okoro

Rector

Oluaka Institute of Technology was agog with the appointment of Frank Okoro (PhD) as the Rector of the Institute.

Dr. Frank Okoro (PhD), a seasoned educationist, has put in record number of years as an educator and administrator. He rose through the ranks in teaching and research in the university system as Head of Department, Dean and Ag. Rector before his appointment and confirmation as Rector of Oluaka Institute of Technology. Dr. Frank has also given his time and services in several portfolios in across the country in various capacity and in the Education ministry portfolio. He is poised to take this citadel of learning to a new level through his efforts in promoting a harmonious relationship between staff of the Institute and the various stakeholders, thus ensuring a stable, productive and predictable academic calendar that is beneficial to all stake holders.
